  • Solera Life sciences

    Solera Pharmaceuticals is one of the leading global pharmaceutical formulation development, manufacturing and marketing companies headquartered in India. At Solera, we strive to provide effective and affordable quality medicines to more people. We create value in the full pharmaceutical value chain from developing to manufacturing and successfully marketing generic pharmaceuticals and we take pride in doing this in an innovative, responsible and cost-efficient way. We offer a product portfolio consisting of a broad range of high quality generics for all major therapeutic areas. It is part of our long-term strategy to expand the product portfolio of off-patent blockbusters while at the same time offering a broader range of niche products within more narrow disease areas.

  • Active Pharma

    Solera products all originate from a generic substance, where the efficacy and safety are well documented. Through our many collaborating partners, we have access to a vast range of technology platforms, enabling us to develop and enhance the intellectual property of our product portfolio. Our strategy of developing products based on well-known substances with an improved value to patients results in a product portfolio with a significant market potential. We achieve this with a relatively short development time, low risk, and limited investment. By focusing on innovation and simplicity in both thought and action, and by taking responsibility on all markets and cost efficiency in all stages, we are creating a strong and vibrant brand that offers quality pharmaceuticals at prices affordable to all.

Our Mission

Solera’s ambition is to become a recognized leader in specialty pharmaceuticals. As a corporate citizen, we aim to take the initiative to address social issues in areas where we can influence our strengths. In this way, Solera’s relationships with society are an integral part of its business development. Our strategy exceeds beyond our core business tactics. We not just develop medicines, but also connect people to resources, facilities and information that improve their well-being and enhance their lives. We are dedicated to forming communal values by developing outstanding pharmaceutical products for one and all. Our innovative R&D and proven ability to manufacture and distribute high-quality pharmaceuticals positions us well for our future in specialty pharmaceuticals.

Innovation-driven Expertise

Innovation and continuous improvement are the critical components in leading Solera towards its goal. Self-induced passion, longing to challenge convention and re-discovering the way we do business only takes us one step closer to achieving the highest standards excellence.

Fulfilling growing needs

We continue to invest and expand globally, with a never-ending search of finding partners for our products in all major markets.

Our mission? To facilitate, manufacture and distribute quality medicines at sustainable prices.

Strength of Collective Partnership

We thrive in a collaborative platform based upon robust partnerships with Manufacturing points and marketing partners. Our approach of evolving strong business-to-business partnerships empowers us to knowledge with partner companies.

Increasing R & D Capabilities

Research and development continue to be vital for the overall value chain of markets and are essential to our company. We, therefore, invest heavily in elementary and functional research. Solera’s strategic R &D roadmap brings science from the lap to patients worldwide.

Our products

APISOL

An anti-inflammatory oral administrative tablets product of Solera. It is a highly effective analgesic product with active ingredients belonging to the class of NSAIDS and antipyretics.

Bonsol

A softgel form orally administrative food- supplementary product of Solera. It is a combination of seven nutrient supplements with the main focus to increase the bones healing period.

CALSOFT -K2-7

A solera anti-helminthic capsule product for oral administration. It also is highly effective in Osteoporosis, Osteoarthritis, Fractures & Joint Pain.This seven-nutrient combination drug increases bone strength by regulating calcium absorption.

CEFISOL 200 DT
